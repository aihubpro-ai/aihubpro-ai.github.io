❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is GST?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is an indirect tax in India. It has 4 components: CGST, SGST, IGST, UTGST.

How to calculate 18% GST?

Formula: GST Amount = (Original Amount × 18) / 100. Example: ₹1000 × 18% = ₹180 GST.

What is difference between CGST and SGST?

CGST goes to Central Govt, SGST goes to State Govt. For intrastate, GST is split 50-50.
